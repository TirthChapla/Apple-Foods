Why Apple Foods ?
With the aim to spread happiness through taste, we are always looking for talent to help us reach our goal. Perks of working with us: Growth opportunities pro max No hierarchy, it’s all about harmony Mondays feel like Fridays here PS: Tons of events with food!

Our flours are manufactured at three state-of-the-art facilities in Rajkot, Chhatral and Junagadh. These three factories are strategically located to cater quickly to the demands from different regions of Gujarat and beyond. These facilities boast of advanced infrastructure and imported machinery for food processing, filtering and quality checking. We upgrade the infrastructure from time to time in order to take advantage of the latest advances in processing technology. We keep ourselves ahead of the curve so that we can scale up and deliver in bulk at a relatively short notice. Our stringent quality-checking parameters ascertain that only top-quality food products get dispatched from our facility so our end customers and institutional clients can experience the joy of good food.

Venu Group...
Our company has been associated with the food industry since 1997 through Gulab Besan. Venu Group was established in 2006 at Chhatral, a renowned industrial town in Gujarat, India, with the objective of making people happy through good food. Now we are a leading manufacturer and supplier of premium quality Besan, Maida, Atta, Rawa and Suji under our flagship brand, Apple.Apple Foods is synonymous in the market with purity, rich taste, high nutritional value, freshness and longer shelf-life.
